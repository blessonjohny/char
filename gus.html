DJ Gus Ballas

DJ Gus Ballas (owner of Ballas Entertainment) is a highly experienced, multi-genre DJ with over 30 years in the entertainment industry, specializing in custom events throughout the New York area. He is widely recognized for his versatility, keeping dance floors packed by seamlessly mixing standard open-format music with deeply specialized cultural genres.

Greek Music Specialist: Deeply rooted in the Greek-American community, playing traditional dances, modern Greek hits, and maintaining the true cultural energy (kefi) required for authentic celebrations.

All-Genre Open Format: Fully versatile across mainstream genres, including Freestyle, Top 40, Classics, Hip Hop, Pop, and Dance music.
