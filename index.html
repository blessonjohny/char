📖 How to Play 28

🎯 Objective

2 teams of 2. First to 12 match points wins.

🃏 Cards

7-Ace only. J=3pts, 9=2pts, A=1pt, 10=1pt. Total 28 points.

📋 Two-Phase Deal

Phase 1: 4 cards, bid from 14. Winner sets trump FACE DOWN.
Phase 2: 4 more cards, bid again from 21 (24+ if rebid).

🎮 Hidden Trump

Trump has NO power until revealed! If you can't follow suit, you can ASK for trump. Then you MUST play a trump if you have one.

⚡ Scoring

≤19: +1/-2 | 20-24: +2/-3 | ≥25: +3/-4
