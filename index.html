📖 How to Play 28
← Menu

🎯 Objective

2 teams of 2. First to 12 match points wins. Each team starts with 6.

🃏 Cards (32 total)

7-Ace only. Jacks=3pts, 9s=2pts, Aces=1pt, 10s=1pt. Total 28 points.

📋 Two-Phase Deal & Bid

Phase 1: Deal 4 cards. Bid from 14. Winner sets trump FACE DOWN.
Phase 2: Deal 4 more. Bid again from 21 (24+ if second bid).

🎮 Play - Hidden Trump Phase

Trump suit has NO power yet! Bidder can't lead trump. If you can't follow suit, you can ASK for trump to be revealed - then you MUST play a trump if you have one.

🎺 Trump Revealed

Once revealed, trump becomes active. Cards of trump suit played BEFORE reveal do NOT count as trumps!

⚡ Scoring

Bid ≤19: Win=+1, Lose=-2. Bid 20-24: Win=+2, Lose=-3. Bid ≥25: Win=+3, Lose=-4.
