About Our Workshop

Welcome to Bolivar Watch & Clock Services, where time-honored craftsmanship meets modern precision. As an independent master watchmaker, I specialize in the meticulous repair and restoration of quartz, automatic, and vintage timepieces.

My state-of-the-art workshop is equipped with top-of-the-line tools and diagnostic instruments, ensuring every repair meets the highest standards of excellence. From routine battery replacements to complex movement overhauls, each timepiece receives the dedicated attention it deserves.

We understand that your watch is more than just an instrument—it is a cherished possession, an heirloom, or a statement of personal style. That is why we guarantee all workmanship and use only genuine parts or high-quality alternatives.

Our Expert Services

🔋

Quartz Watch Repair

Battery replacement, circuit diagnostics, coil repairs, and complete quartz movement servicing. Fast turnaround with precision testing.

⚙️

Automatic Watch Service

Full movement overhaul, lubrication, regulation, and water resistance testing. Restoring your automatic timepiece to factory specifications.

🏛️

Vintage Restoration

Specialized restoration of antique and vintage timepieces. Preserving historical integrity while ensuring reliable timekeeping.

🕐

Clock Repair

Wall clocks, mantel clocks, grandfather clocks, and specialty timepieces. Mechanical and quartz clock expertise.

💎

Case & Bracelet Refinishing

Professional polishing, scratch removal, and bracelet adjustment. Restoring the original luster of your timepiece.

🌊

Water Resistance Testing

Pressure testing and seal replacement to ensure your watch maintains its rated water resistance specifications.
